Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others. Please find the data dictionary in the link below data dictionary

Distribution of LoanStatus¶

More than 50,000 borrowers have their loans as current, more than 30,000 have their loans completed and approximately less than a 1,000 have them as due and cancelled.

Distribution of ListingCategory¶

The main objecjective of this plot is to find the reason for borrowers to take the loans. It is therefore observed that debt consolidation was the main reason as indicated but a count of above 50,000. However, there were other reasons such as homeimprovement, business, personal loan etc which had a relative low count of less than 10,000. From the distribution, Rv was the least reason as it appears to the far right side of the graph.

LoanStatus and StatedMonthlyIncome¶

The bar graph below illustrates the relationship of LoanStatus to StatedMonthlyIncome and we can say that borrowers with the higher monthly income have their loan at the FinalPayment in progress. Those who have the least monthly income have their loans as cancelled due to various factors such as high interest rates which made it hard for them to pay the loans hence cancelling.

MonthlyLoanPayment, LoanOriginalAmount and EmploymentStatus¶

As observed from the plot blow, the higher the loan original amount acquired, the higher the monthly payment of the loan accross all the employment status categories. We cana also conclude that most borrowers who are self employed,employed,full-time, and in other categories took loans thant ranges from 0 to 25,000 while the non-employed, part-time and the retired borrowed amount ranging from 0 to 15,000 and their monthly loan payments were lower.
